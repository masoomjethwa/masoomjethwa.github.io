üéØ How to Play:

  1. Setup: 2+ teams, each chooses a rocket piece. Place all pieces on START.
  2. Turn: Draw a card from the deck matching your space color (or any color on normal spaces).
  3. Draw: You have 60 seconds to draw the term WITHOUT words, letters, or numbers.
  4. Guess: Your team tries to guess. If correct, roll die and move forward.
  5. Special Spaces: Black Holes = skip turn, Nebulas = draw advanced cards.
  6. Win: First team to reach the Galactic Core wins!

üìù Setup Instructions:

  • Print: Print all pages on standard 8.5" x 11" paper
  • Board: The game board is ready to use as-is
  • Cards: Cut out each word card along the border lines
  • Game Pieces: Use small objects like coins, buttons, or toy rockets
  • Timer: Use a phone timer or watch for 60-second rounds
  • Drawing Materials: Provide paper and pencils/pens for each team

üéÆ Pro Tips:

  • Shuffle cards well and keep categories in separate piles
  • For younger players, allow 90 seconds instead of 60
  • Create house rules for "close enough" answers
  • Advanced players can try drawing with their non-dominant hand!
