Despite over six decades of intensive planetary exploration, our solar system continues to guard profound mysteries across all its scales‚Äîfrom the Sun's fiery core to the distant, icy reaches of the Oort Cloud. This infographic poster synthesizes the major unsolved or poorly understood phenomena related to:

  • The Sun and its dynamic processes.

  • Small bodies like asteroids and comets.
  • The enigmatic Kuiper Belt and Oort Cloud.
  • The very edge of the interstellar medium (heliopause).

‚úÖ Conclusion: The Unfolding Cosmos

Despite vast achievements in space exploration, our solar system still holds profound mysteries about its origin, evolution, and the diversity of its worlds. Addressing these unresolved questions demands:

  • Cross-disciplinary modeling.
  • Robust international collaboration.
  • Long-duration exploration beyond current frontiers.

Unlocking these secrets will not only reshape our understanding of the solar system but also profoundly inform our search for life beyond Earth.
