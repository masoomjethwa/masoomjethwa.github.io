✨ Theories of Cosmic Origin ✨

An interactive explorer of the scientific hypotheses about the formation of our Solar System.

The quest to understand our origins is a story of evolving ideas. Early theories imagined a single, self-contained genesis, while later concepts introduced dramatic cosmic interactions. Use the tabs below to explore these distinct categories of thought and see how scientific understanding has progressed.

A Timeline of Thought

This timeline visualizes the evolution of these major theories from the 18th century to the mid-20th century. Hover over any point to see details. Notice the long gap before the burst of new ideas in the 1900s, driven by advances in physics and astronomy.

This interactive application was generated to provide an engaging overview of complex scientific history.
